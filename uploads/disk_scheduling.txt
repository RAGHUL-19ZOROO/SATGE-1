Disk scheduling and storage structure:

- Disks store data on tracks and sectors.
- Disk access time depends on seek time, rotational latency, and transfer time.
- Disk scheduling improves performance by ordering disk I/O requests efficiently.

Algorithms:
- FCFS
- SSTF
- SCAN
- C-SCAN
- LOOK

Goal:
- Reduce head movement and improve throughput.
